Nathan Boshart

Photographer and Videographer

My name is Nathan and I am from Kitchener, ON. I got my first camera in 2016 and since then I have been enthralled with photography. I enjoy reading and learning about different aspects of photography and putting my knowledge to practical use.

My Video Work

Guelph Wellington Paramedic Service Real BVM Help - Training Video

Worked for Guelph Wellington Paramedic Service. Shot and produced training video for Zoll Real BVM Help module for Zoll defibrillator. This is the first training video ever made on this product. This video is currently used by ZOLL Medical Corporation for training of medical professionals throughout North America.

Lifting Assignment Demonstration - Conestoga College Paramedic Program

Hired by Conestoga College Paramedic Program Coordinator. Edited and produced video demonstration of a lifting assignment. Utilized to demonstrate the lifting mechanics assessment for the program. Additionally, aided the Program Coordinator in uploading this video to Youtube for her students to view it.

HHSS Charity Auction 2019

Volunteered to edit this video for the charity auction at my high school. Took all photographs and shot all the videos in the finished product. Edited the pictures prior to compiling them into the video. Edited all pictures and videos together into a logical and polished final product. Used creative direction to tell the story of the charity auction through video and photographs.
